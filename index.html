about me

I began my career at a global human rights organization as Program Manager and Technology Coordinator. Working with tech innovators from all over the world inspired me to transition to a career in web development. I am a full-stack developer with experience building creative and dynamic web applications using a variety of platforms, frameworks, and languages.


Currently, I am located in Brooklyn, New York, but I make sure to escape the city as often as I can. In addition to my love of travel, I also enjoy playing soccer, landscape photography, and woodworking.
