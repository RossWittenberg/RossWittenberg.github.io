about me

I am full-stack web developer with a project management background and an eye for clean, functional design. I build dynamic web applications using a variety of platforms, frameworks, and languages. For me, making website and writing code is about balancing logical problem-solving and critical thinking with imagination and creativity.


Prior to web development, I worked as the program and production manager of the Oslo Freedom Forum—an international human rights summit held annually in Norway. In this capacity, I worked alongside with innovators and technologists from around the world. These experiences inspired to transition to a career in tech.


Currently, I am located in Brooklyn, New York, but I make sure to escape the city in search of nature and adventure as often as I can. In addition to my love of programming and travel, I also enjoy playing soccer, landscape photography, and woodworking.
