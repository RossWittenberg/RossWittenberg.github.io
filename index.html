I am full-stack web developer with a project management background and an eye for clean, functional design. I build dynamic web applications using a variety of platforms, frameworks, and languages. For me, creating websites and writing code is a balance between logic and creativity and the intersection of critical thinking and imagination.


Prior to web development, I worked with a human rights organization as the program and production manager for the Oslo Freedom Forum—an international human rights summit held annually in Norway. I worked alongside innovators and technologists from around the world and was exposed to alternative understandings and perspectives on how to create, build, and design. It was these fundamental experiences that inspired me to transition to a career in tech.


Currently, I am located in Brooklyn, New York, but I make sure to escape the city in search of nature and adventure as often as I can. In addition to my passion for programming, I enjoy travel, playing soccer, landscape photography, and woodworking.
